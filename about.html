I wanted to solve problems

A large part of being an artist is focused on telling your story and sharing your “very important thoughts” with the rest of world.

I realized that I didn’t want to have a career that was so focused on my own self fulfillment, but I still wanted a job that was in the creative field.

Stumbling into UX

I found UX and it was love at first sight. I love using my creativity to work on interesting problems.

I love being able to approach design like a scientist. As a UX designer, I get to identify a problem, research and design a solution, and then validate my solution through testing.

Starting my education in traditional art

My whole life, I thought I wanted to be an artist. So when I returned from a two year service trip to France in 2019, I started the Illustration program at Utah Valley University.

I loved creating beautiful pieces of art, however I quickly became disillusioned with the art industry.

In my spare time…

I am probably hanging out with my wonderful friends, DJing, designing little houses in Minecraft, playing pickleball, listening to my favorite music, or reading a good book.
